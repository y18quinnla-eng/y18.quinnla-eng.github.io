About

Unfiltered Healing is a disability-led project raising awareness about FND, epilepsy, mental health, and hidden disabilities. We share real experiences, education, and safe spaces so no one feels alone.

Our Mission

  • Educate people about misunderstood conditions
  • Amplify disabled voices
  • Create safe online communities
  • Support accessibility and independence
